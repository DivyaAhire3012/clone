ACM Winter School 2023: An Immersive Journey into Data Science for Social Good From January 11th to January 22th, I had the privilege of attending the ACM Winter School 2023 on Data Science for Social Goods, hosted by ACM at IIT Delhi, India. This immersive program provided a unique platform to explore the intersection of data science and its role in addressing pressing social challenges. ✨Over the course of two weeks, we delved into a wide range of topics, including ethical considerations in data handling, machine learning applications for social good, and the potential of data-driven approaches to tackle issues such as healthcare accessibility, education equity, and environmental sustainability. Through engaging lectures, hands-on workshops, and interactive discussions, we gained valuable insights into harnessing data science for positive social impact. ✨One of the highlights of the winter school was the opportunity to collaborate on real-world projects aimed at making a tangible difference in communities. Working in interdisciplinary teams, we applied our newly acquired skills to develop innovative solutions and prototypes tailored to specific social challenges. This experience was not only intellectually stimulating but also deeply rewarding, as we witnessed firsthand the transformative power of data science in driving positive change. ✨I am immensely grateful to the esteemed faculty members and industry experts who shared their expertise and passion for leveraging technology for social impact. Their mentorship was instrumental in shaping our understanding and inspiring us to aspire for meaningful contributions to society. The program featured esteemed guest speakers. -prof.Arun Rajkumar [IIT Madras] -prof.Dweepobotee Brahma Brahma [IIT Jodhpur] -prof. Ponnurangam Kumaraguru (PK) [IIIT Hyderabad] -Dr. Srujana Merugu [Amazon] -prof.Anirban Dasgupta [IIT Gandhinagar] Dr.Abhijnan Chakraborty [IIT Delhi] I extended my heartfelt gratitude to ACM India and SVKM's Institute of Technology, Dhule for providing me with this opportunity I would like to express my heartfelt appreciation to the organizers, sponsors, and supporters who made this winter school possible. Their dedication to advancing the field of data science for social goods is truly commendable, and I am honored to have been a part of this remarkable program. hashtag#Svkm hashtag#acmstudentchapter hashtag#ACMindia hashtag#srmuniversity hashtag#datascience
